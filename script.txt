# IELTS Speaking Section 1: Renting an Apartment

**[F] Sarah:** Hello, I'm Sarah from the housing office. What's your name?

**[M] James:** Hi Sarah, I'm James. Thanks for meeting with me today.

**[F] Sarah:** No problem, James. So, I understand you're looking for an apartment to rent. Tell me a bit about what you need.

**[M] James:** Yes, that's right. I'm relocating to the city for work next month, and I need somewhere to live. I'm looking for a one-bedroom place, ideally close to the city center.

**[F] Sarah:** I see. And what's your budget? How much are you willing to spend monthly?

**[M] James:** Um... I'd say around 1,200 to 1,500 dollars a month would be ideal.

**[F] Sarah:** That's reasonable. Are there any other requirements? For example, do you need parking, or is public transport convenient for you?

**[M] James:** Well, parking would be nice, but it's not essential since I use the metro for work. However, I do prefer a place with good natural light and maybe a small balcony if possible.

**[F] Sarah:** Good to know. Those are quite specific. How long are you planning to stay? Are you looking for a short-term or long-term lease?

**[M] James:** Definitely long-term. I'm planning to stay here for at least two years, maybe longer. I'd like stability, you know?

**[F] Sarah:** Absolutely, that helps. Now, have you rented an apartment before?

**[M] James:** Yes, I have. I rented a studio apartment for three years before this. So I'm familiar with the rental process...

**[F] Sarah:** That's helpful experience. One more thing—do you have references from your previous landlord?

**[M] James:** Yes, I do. My former landlord was very satisfied with me as a tenant. I always paid rent on time and kept the place in good condition.

**[F] Sarah:** Excellent. I think I have several apartments that might suit you. When would you like to view them?

**[M] James:** How about next Saturday? I'm free in the afternoon.

**[F] Sarah:** Perfect! I'll arrange that for you and send you the details by email.

**[M] James:** Great, thank you so much for your help!
