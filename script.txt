# IELTS Listening Test Section 1

**[F] Sarah:** Good morning, thank you for calling The Riverside Hotel. How may I assist you today?

**[M] James:** Hello, yes, I'd like to make a reservation please. I'm looking for accommodation for next month, around the 15th through the 22nd of June.

**[F] Sarah:** Of course. Let me check our availability for those dates. That's eight nights, correct?

**[M] James:** Yes, that's right. I need a double room, preferably with a view if possible.

**[F] Sarah:** Excellent. We do have several options available. Our double rooms with garden views are £95 per night, whilst our rooms overlooking the river are £125 per night.

**[M] James:** The river view sounds nice. What's included in that rate?

**[F] Sarah:** The river-view rooms include full English breakfast, complimentary WiFi, and access to our fitness centre. We also offer free parking if you're driving.

**[M] James:** That's brilliant. Can I ask... do you have any special packages or discounts available?

**[F] Sarah:** Actually, we're currently offering 10% off for bookings over seven nights. Given your eight-night stay, you'd save quite a bit. That would bring it to £112.50 per night.

**[M] James:** That's great value. And would I need to pay a deposit?

**[F] Sarah:** We require a 25% non-refundable deposit to confirm the booking. However, cancellation is free if you give us at least two weeks' notice.

**[M] James:** That's reasonable. Could I get those terms in writing?

**[F] Sarah:** Absolutely. I'll need your email address. Also, what name should the reservation be under?

**[M] James:** It's James Mitchell. My email is james.mitchell.74@email.com.

**[F] Sarah:** Perfect. And a contact telephone number?

**[M] James:** 07700 956428.

**[F] Sarah:** Lovely. I'll email you the booking confirmation along with payment details. Your check-in is from 3 PM onwards, and check-out is at 11 AM on the 22nd. Is there anything else I can help with?

**[M] James:** Just one thing – is there a restaurant on-site?

**[F] Sarah:** Yes, we have The Riverside Bistro, open from 6 to 10 PM daily. I'd recommend booking ahead during peak season. Shall I make you a reservation?

**[M] James:** Perhaps for the first evening? Say, the 15th at 7 PM?

**[F] Sarah:** Wonderful. How many guests?

**[M] James:** Just myself, actually.

**[F] Sarah:** Noted. I'll arrange that for you. You'll have a fantastic stay with us, Mr. Mitchell.

**[M] James:** Thank you so much for your help.

**[F] Sarah:** You're most welcome. Enjoy The Riverside Hotel!
