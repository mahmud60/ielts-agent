# IELTS Questions Based on Renting an Apartment Conversation

## 1. MULTIPLE CHOICE QUESTIONS

**Question 1:** What is James's primary reason for relocating to the city?
- A) To study at a university
- B) For a work opportunity
- C) To be closer to family
- D) To experience city life

**Answer: B**

**Question 2:** Which of the following is NOT mentioned as one of James's apartment requirements?
- A) One bedroom
- B) Close to city center
- C) Air conditioning
- D) Good natural light

**Answer: C**

**Question 3:** What does James use for commuting to work?
- A) His personal car
- B) Public transport (metro)
- C) Bicycle
- D) Walking

**Answer: B**

**Question 4:** How long does James plan to stay in the apartment?
- A) One year
- B) Six months
- C) At least two years
- D) Three years

**Answer: C**

---

## 2. FILL IN THE BLANKS

**Question 5:** James's monthly budget for rent is between $_______ and $_______ dollars.

**Answer: 1,200; 1,500**

**Question 6:** James previously rented a _______ apartment for three years before this.

**Answer: studio**

**Question 7:** James prefers a place with good _______ light and a small _______.

**Answer: natural; balcony**

**Question 8:** James mentions that his former landlord was very _______ with him as a tenant.

**Answer: satisfied**

---

## 3. TRUE/FALSE/NOT GIVEN

**Question 9:** James requires parking as an essential feature for the apartment.
- A) True
- B) False
- C) Not Given

**Answer: B (False)** - He states parking would be nice but is not essential.

**Question 10:** Sarah offers to show James the apartments on Saturday morning.
- A) True
- B) False
- C) Not Given

**Answer: B (False)** - James is free in the afternoon on Saturday, and Sarah agrees to arrange that time.

---

**BONUS SHORT ANSWER QUESTION:**

**Question 11:** According to James, what important quality did he demonstrate as a previous tenant?
- **Answer:** He always paid rent on time and kept the place in good condition (or any variation mentioning punctual payment and property maintenance).
