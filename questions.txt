# IELTS Listening Test – Section 1: Question Set

**Topic: Renting an Apartment**

---

## PART A: Multiple Choice
*Choose the correct letter, A, B, or C.*

**1.** Why does Sarah need a furnished apartment?
- A. She sold all her furniture before moving
- B. She recently relocated to the city and has no furniture
- C. She prefers furnished apartments for financial reasons

**2.** How much is the monthly rent for the apartment on Hartfield Road?
- A. £900
- B. £950
- C. £850

**3.** Which bills are included in the monthly rent?
- A. Water and electricity
- B. Water only
- C. Electricity and internet

**4.** How long is the minimum rental contract?
- A. Three months
- B. Six months
- C. Twelve months

**5.** When is the viewing appointment scheduled?
- A. Thursday at 2:00 PM
- B. Friday at 2:00 PM
- C. Thursday at 3:00 PM

---

## PART B: Fill in the Blanks
*Complete the sentences below using **NO MORE THAN TWO WORDS AND/OR A NUMBER** from the recording.*

**6.** The apartment is located on the __________ floor, and the building has a __________ for residents.

**7.** The security deposit is equal to __________ weeks' rent and is held in a __________ scheme.

**8.** The nearest tube station is called __________ and is only a __________ walk from the apartment.

**9.** The apartment becomes available from the __________ of next month, and Sarah must submit her __________ and references beforehand.

**10.** Sarah's contact phone number is __________.

---

## PART C: True / False / Not Given
*Write **TRUE** if the statement agrees with the information, **FALSE** if it contradicts the information, or **NOT GIVEN** if there is no information about this.*

**11.** The apartment includes two parking spaces in the underground car park.

**12.** Internet service is available in the building but must be activated separately.

**13.** Sarah intends to use the second bedroom as a guest room.

**14.** The supermarket near the apartment is approximately a two-minute walk away.

**15.** Mr. Collins has personally visited the apartment on Hartfield Road.

## PART D: Short Answer Questions
*Answer the questions below using **NO MORE THAN THREE WORDS** from the recording.*

**17.** Where is the storage unit located within the building?

**18.** What is located directly outside the apartment building?

**19.** How long a contract do most tenants typically sign?
